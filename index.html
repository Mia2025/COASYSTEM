The law is specific — and it is on your side

Florida's Condominium Act gives every owner the right to inspect the association's official records within 10 business days of a written request.

"The failure of an association to provide the records within 10 working days after receipt of a written request creates a rebuttable presumption that the association willfully failed to comply… minimum damages of $50 per calendar day (up to 10 days)." — Section 718.111(12), Florida Statutes (summary)
